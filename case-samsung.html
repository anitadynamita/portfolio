The Frame · Privacy Epic Tips

Two Samsung products, one storytelling discipline: The Frame, the TV that becomes art when it’s off, and the Galaxy S24 Ultra with its privacy and AI features. Both campaigns ran as vertical social film for the German market in 2024.

01Objective

Turn product features, customisable bezels, matte display, Auto Blocker, on-device AI, into something worth stopping a scroll for.

02Idea

One story per feature: series grammar instead of one-off spots, so each film compounds the campaign instead of competing with it.

03Execution

Motion for both series: UI and product demonstrations woven seamlessly into live-action footage, each story paced so the demo never breaks the narrative, finished to Samsung’s exacting brand standards.

04Result

Two series shipped in 2024, The Frame’s gallery-calm films and Epic Tips’ lifestyle episodes, one consistent visual grammar across both.
