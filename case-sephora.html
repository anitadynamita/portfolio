SEPHORA · PUBLICIS · RETAIL MEDIA

One framework, every brand

A motion system for Sephora's brand-partner campaigns, different beauty houses, different markets, one recognisable Sephora craft.

Year

2023 – 2025

Industry

Beauty · Retail

Work

Motion Design, Creative Production, Localisation

Markets

DE · CH · FR · SE · DK

Sephora’s retail media runs on partnerships: every season, houses like Too Faced, Sol de Janeiro, NARS, Rare Beauty, Benefit and KOSAS launch exclusively at Sephora, and each launch needs paid social across five European markets.

01Objective

Make every launch feel unmistakably like the partner brand and unmistakably like Sephora, across DE, CH, FR, SE and DK, with each market’s language and legal requirements intact.

02Idea

One motion framework flexible enough to absorb any house’s visual codes without diluting Sephora’s own signature, brand design thinking applied to a production system.

03Execution

Animated product campaigns built inside the shared framework: each brand’s art direction honoured, Sephora’s lockups and rhythm constant, then carried across markets, formats (9:16, 1:1) and legal footers without losing craft.

04Result

Six-plus brand partners, five markets, season after season, a system that held because it was built to absorb whatever the next launch brought.

One framework, four houses >

Four brands, four visual worlds, same underlying system. Swipe-stopping product motion tailored to each house's codes, wearing Sephora's signature throughout.
