Welltech builds some of the world’s most downloaded health & fitness apps. For Muscle Booster’s female calisthenics campaigns, paid social burns through creative faster than any team can produce it, this week’s winning ad is next week’s fatigued one.

01Objective

Scale user-acquisition creative for Meta, TikTok and Instagram without scaling headcount, and outrun creative fatigue instead of chasing it.

02Idea

Don’t make ads faster; build the system that makes them. Treat every creative as a hypothesis and every launch as an experiment.

03Execution

A pipeline of my own design: After Effects expressions and scripting fused with AI video, voice and music tools (Veo, Sora, Higgsfield, Runway, ElevenLabs, Suno). One winning concept becomes 20–100 localised, test-ready variations, subtitles, colour and formats automated, creative decisions human.

04Result

31% ROI on $74K+ media spend, driven by continuous iteration and A/B testing, and a production culture where data decides which creative wins.
